Research Projects

These are some of my notable research projects

  • ml_astro_boss

    Another student and I trained a Deep Learning Model to predict the stellar parameters of stars. This pipeline is now being used for the inference of new stars by other researchers in the field for stars observed by the BOSS telescope.

    STATUS: Publication is currently in review.

  • tronn

    Two other students and I were tasked with benchmarking a novel Deep Learning Algorithm (DPC) against a littanny of other more traditional styles of Deep Reinforcement Learning. We tested these algorithms in 6 different environments with different levels of true system interaction to test the data efficiency of each algorithm

    STATUS: Abstract submitted.

  • ml_astro_hydra

    Three other students and I are working on developing a pipeline for estimating the stellar parameters observed by any telescope, agnostic to any artifacts that telescope may have.

    STATUS: In progress.
